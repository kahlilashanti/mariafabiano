Services

Counseling

Available for youth and adults struggling with substance use issues, trauma, anxiety, depression, grief and loss. My approach is integrative, collaborative, and customized. My work is influenced by mindfulness based interventions, somatic psychotherapy, cognitive based therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT), solution focused, and narrative therapy.

Yoga
Private and Public

With 20 years of personal practice and study of both yoga and Buddhist philosophy, I pay particular attention to alignment, borrowed from my practice of Iyengar yoga. Expect a fluid, flowing style that integrates both mindfulness and the breath, similar to Ashtanga yoga.
